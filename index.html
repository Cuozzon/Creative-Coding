Retro Television Clock

A retro TV clock where time is shown using colored balls that fall from the top of the screen, with the largest representing hours, the middle minutes, and the smallest seconds. As the balls descend, their colors fade, and they reset to the top after completing a full hour, minute, or 60 seconds.

Trail Walker

"Trail Walker" is a single-button hiking-themed game where the player is represented by an orange backpack. The objective of the game is to dodge trees and collect compasses and maps for points. If you hit a tree, the game is over.

Sylvia's 50,000 Words

A book inspired by Sylvia Plath, using the titles of all her poems as sentences arranged in random order to give a unique experience on each read. Each chapter is named "Sylvia," and the book is designed with an old, typewriter-like aesthetic to reflect her writing style. I created the title, The Fox with the Glass Teeth, to encompass the feel of her poetry.
